About Skyron

Skyron is an Indian-born luxury sneaker brand that merges top-tier Chinese craftsmanship with bold Indian identity. Our mission is to redefine how India experiences streetwear and premium sneakers — by offering limited-edition, no-repeat styles directly to consumers who crave uniqueness and quality.

We manufacture in select Chinese factories that produce for the world’s biggest luxury labels. Every Skyron pair is produced in strictly limited quantities — typically just 50 to 100 pairs per drop — giving each owner the confidence that they're wearing something no one else in their city likely has.

Our design philosophy is global, but our spirit is unmistakably Indian. Whether it’s our collector edition cards, drop packaging, or our pricing model — we aim to deliver luxury without elitism.

Skyron is more than a shoe — it's a movement for India's next generation of creators, rebels, and originals.
