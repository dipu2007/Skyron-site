Meet the Founder: Deepak P. Behera

Deepak P. Behera is the visionary behind Skyron Footwear, blending innovation and futuristic design from his hometown in Farukh Nagar, Haryana. With a passion for wearable technology and creative comfort, Deepak:

  • Crafted his first prototypes by hand in 2018
  • Collaborated with material scientists on thermochromic and kinetic energy technologies
  • Secured multiple patents on smart-footwear innovations
  • Hosts community-driven design hackathons across India

Through his blog and live Q&A sessions, Deepak inspires the next generation of footwear entrepreneurs.

About Skyron

Skyron is a limited-edition sneaker brand proudly founded in India, combining precision Chinese craftsmanship with bold Indian street culture. Our goal is to deliver collectible-quality sneakers that spark confidence and stand apart in a world full of copycats.

Each drop is released in strictly limited numbers — sometimes just 50 to 100 pairs — so you always wear something rare. From our aggressive design silhouettes to packaging that feels premium, everything is built for sneaker lovers who value quality over quantity.

Skyron is not just about shoes. It’s about voice. It’s about representing Indian youth who want to move different. Dream global. And wear loud.
